## **STASH FINANCIAL ENGINEERING TRANSFORMATION** - VP of Engineering

**THE CHALLENGE**
* **Platform Crisis:** 15+ hours monthly downtime impacting 2M+ users; OAuth failures and back-end service crashes and native app bugs causing regular customer lockouts, many requiring app restarts
* **Technical Chaos:** Six+ stalled mid-migration projects (feature flags, three partially implemented secrets management vendors, Heroku to AWS, Fargate to EKS, etc); 30% incomplete in-house UX Design System tripling feature costs; mixed Ruby/Go/Scala/Java architecture with no company standards
* **Organizational Dysfunction:** 220 engineers in director-level silos with jerrymandered ownership determined by Product Management; "toxically positive" culture preventing candid feedback; weak technical leadership unable to drive lasting, company-wide decisions
* **Delivery Paralysis:** 3-month iOS/Android release cycles; organization skilled at starting projects but often unable to finish
* **Business Impact:** Stagnant customer growth, high CAC, flat ARPU in highly regulated fintech environment (SOC-2 Type II, FINRA, FDIC)

**MY APPROACH**
* **Progressive Leadership Expansion:** Grew scope from 6→40→220 engineers; assumed interim CTO responsibilities after leadership change; became trusted technical voice across entire organization
* **Cultural Transformation:** Instituted Amazon LP-inspired principles (Ownership, Customer Centricity, Delivering Results, etc); created OE MVP program with weekly training; wrote and ghost-wrote content to drive change resulting in SLIs/SLOs/dashboards via cross-team best-practices sharing
* **Technical Standardization:** Mandated Go as single backend language; eliminated Scala/Java fragmentation; established versioned APIs replacing ad-hoc Ruby endpoints, zero down-time deployments
* **SRE & Operational Excellence:** Partnered with sole Staff SRE to elevate discipline to reliability engineering leaders (dotted-line OE MVPs per team); implemented comprehensive Datadog monitoring and COE continuous improvement process
* **Leadership Rebuilding:** Hired Engineering Director partner; promoted internal talent; designed improved org structure; reduced overall headcount by 50%; retained key Principal Engineer who had been a long-time flight risk and became the org's chief architect

**MEASURABLE RESULTS**
* **Reliability:** Platform downtime reduced 60% (15→6 hours monthly) through proper monitoring and incident management
* **Cost Efficiency:** AWS costs reduced 45% despite ongoing growth; improved OpEx ratio from ~55% to 30% of revenue
* **Delivery Velocity:** Mobile release cycles accelerated from 3 months to 5 weeks; completed six stalled migration projects
* **Team Transformation:** Built fungible backend engineering team with full ownership, including on-call and deployments
* **Design System:** Increased coverage from 70% to functional completion, dramatically reducing feature implementation costs
* **Regulatory Compliance:** Maintained 100% compliance across SOC-2 Type II, FINRA, and FDIC requirements throughout transformation

**LASTING IMPACT**
Created sustainable engineering culture in complex fintech environment serving underbanked Americans. Transformed organization from reactive firefighting to proactive ownership model. Retained and empowered high-potential engineers while clearing organizational blockers. Engineering transformation enabled reliable service delivery for 2M+ users managing their financial futures, providing the technical foundation for future business growth.
